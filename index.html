01 / About

The short version.

A frontend developer who treats motion, type, and spacing as load-bearing — not decoration.

I'm Umer — a frontend developer focused on building interfaces that feel considered: fast to load, clear to use, and unmistakably polished. My work sits at the intersection of engineering discipline and visual craft.

I care about the details most users won't consciously notice — the easing on a hover state, the rhythm of a grid, the weight of a typeface — because that's what separates a site that works from one that feels inevitable.

Outside of client work, I'm usually deep in a side project exploring some corner of motion design or performance, trying to close the gap between what a browser can do and what most sites actually do.
